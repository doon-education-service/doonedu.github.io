What Makes Us Special?

Recent Awards

"DIS was awarded for 'INNOVATIVE PRACTICES FOR ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE' at the WORLD EDUCATION AWARDS ceremony organised by DIGITAL LEARNING MAGAZINE on August 10th 2018 at New Delhi wherein 37 schools and 7 universities from around the world were awarded in different categories."

"'TOP SCHOOLS OF INDIA' Award by Elets Adding another feather to our cap, Doon International School was conferred the 'TOP SCHOOLS OF INDIA' award by Elets at the 6th School Leadership Summit held on 24th February, 2018, at Eros Hotel in New Delhi."

"Doon International School has again carved its presence by winning the National School Award 2019 under two categories

(b) Commendable contribution in changing the education system"
